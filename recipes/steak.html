Steak

Description

Treat yourself sometimes

The luxury of a well-made steak is undeniable.Belying a simplicity, the reverse seared steak will truly make you feel like a king.

Ingredients

  • Beef sirloin 250g
  • Onions 100g
  • Mushrooms 100g
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Rosemary to taste
  • Thyme to taste
  • Butter 2tbsp
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the sirloin generously with salt and pepper on each side and let rest for 2 hours, uncovered.
  2. Preheat oven to 250F, oil a roasting pan and add in the steak.
  3. Roast the steak for 30 minutes, flipping halfway.
  4. Remove steak from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  5. In a pan sautee the onions and mushrooms, season with salt.
  6. Sear the steak on all sides in the pan.
  7. Add the butter and herbs to pan, baste the steak in the melted butter.
  8. Let the steak cool for 5 minutes before serving.
