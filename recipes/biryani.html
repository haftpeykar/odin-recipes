Biryani

Description

Bring out the Karachi in you.

A kingly dish that traces the aromas of saffron, cardammom and chilli within succulent meat and fluffy rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken 500g
  • Tomatoes 250g
  • Onions 250g
  • Shan Biryani Masala
  • Rice 500g

Steps

  1. Set rice to boil at a low heat, with some salt.
  2. Once the rice is parboiled set it aside.
  3. In a pan fry onions until lightly browned, add the meat and stir fry for five minutes.
  4. Add the Biryani Masala and continue to cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add the tomatoes and turn down the heat, simmer for 30 minutes until reduced.
  6. Add rice to the mixture, let steam for 15 minutes, then serve.
